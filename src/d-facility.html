Description

BELMONT ALBANY VILLAGE is the only Level One trauma center in Berkeley and Northern San Mateo County. We provide life-saving medical care to the 1.5 million adults and children of Berkeley, San Francisco and northern San Mateo County. We offer comprehensive tertiary services designed to meet the needs of diverse communities. Services include 24-hour emergency care, minimally invasive surgery, high risk obstetrics and Level III Newborn Intensive Care Nursery
